Angus Montgomery

Angus Montgomery is a writer, editor and creative strategist. He currently works for the Government Digital Service where he works with teams to help them articulate their work. He was previously editor of Design Week and managing editor of the Architects' Journal. He regularly writes, interviews, talks and works on films and other projects on subjects including design, technology and education.

Twitter Linkedin Github Instagram

Current work

Government Digital Service

Working with teams across the Government Digital Service, helping them to define and articulate their work. Developing strategies and comms plans as well as writing presentations, blog posts and articles. Working on projects including GOV.UK, Government as a Platform and Service Design and Standards.
